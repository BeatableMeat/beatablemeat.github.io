About Beatable Meat

The Beatable Meat project is run by The Phoenix Project, Heatwave Studios and Bryson Records. The Phoenix Project is the creator of the Beatable Meat Portal, Heatwave Studios is the creator of the main website and Beatable Games, Bryson Records is the creator of Bruh-o-Meter 2020 and GlitchGod is the creator of GlitchGod's flash repo. The editors of the sites are @sabian-heatwave on Github and @daniel-heatwave on Github.
