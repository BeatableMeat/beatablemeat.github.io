A Notice About This Screenplay

This is the theatre play version of The Room, written in 1999. It has many differences compared to the 2003 version.

1. Denny is called Billy.
2. Billy is Lisa's brother, not revealed in the 2003 film.
3. Billy is homosexual. Lisa's mother, Claudette, disowns him because of this.
4. The drug dealer has a name: Jimmy!
5. In the movie, Johnny and Mark get hold of the drug dealer and take him down to the police station. In this screenplay, Jimmy threatens Lisa and Claudette.
6. Jimmy is a drug-dealer in the film. In this version, Jimmy is Billy's boss. Billy is a prostitute.
7. Billy finds him self sexually attracted to Johnny.
